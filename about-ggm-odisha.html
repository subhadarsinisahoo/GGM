About COTR

Grace Gospel Ministries, (A Pentecostal Charismatic Movement to reach the unreached), is a registered organization, regiered as trust in Berhampur, Odisha. It started under the leadership of Rev. Varughese Cherian with the great vision which God has given to him after the death of Australian missionary Graham Stains and his two sons Timothy and Philip and also with much Fasting and Prayers.

Grace Gospel Ministries, (A Pentecostal Charismatic Movement to reach the unreached) Regd. No. 189/08/IV have its FCRA and 12A.

GGM is the Bible-based, Christ-centered and Mission-oriented ministry. GGM is fully committed to win Orissa/India for Christ. Through a multi-faceted ministry of formal and informal training, evangelistic outreach, church planting, medical missions, literature publication and distribution and social welfare projects such as caring for orphans, widows, and physically handicapped, we obey and fulfill the Great Commission of Jesus Christ “Go preach and make disciples”.
