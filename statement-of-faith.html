Statement of Faith

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and is authoritative and infallible in the writings.

We believe in one God, who revealed Himself in three persons: God the father, God the Son and God the Holy Spirit.

We believe in the virgin birth of Jesus, the deity of Jesus Christ, sinless life, miracles, and death on the cross to provide for our redemption, bodily resurrection and ascension into heaven, present ministry of intercession for us, and His return to earth in power and glory.

We believe in the personality and deity of the Holy Spirit, that He performs the miracle of the new birth in an unbeliever and indwells believers, enabling them to live a godly life filled with power.

We believe that man was created in the image of God, but because of sin, was alienated from God. That alienation can be removed only by accepting through faith God’s gift of salvation, which was made possible by Christ’s death.
